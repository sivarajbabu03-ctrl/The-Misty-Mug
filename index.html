Our Story

Born in the Hills,
Brewed with Heart

High up in the Nilgiri Mountains, where morning mist rolls through eucalyptus forests and the air carries the scent of fresh tea leaves — The Misty Mug was born from a simple love for honest, soul-warming food and drink.


From hand-picked Nilgiri teas to house-made Ooty chocolates, everything we serve tells the story of these incredible hills. Come, sit by the window, watch the mist, and let us take care of the rest.
